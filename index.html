How to introduce yourself as a student

Introduction is basically telling someone about yourself whom you meet for the first time. There are many ways to introduce or represent yourself but i am going to teach you the basic way. It is very important for a student to indroduce him or herself properly and in a organized way.

Your introduction (For a Student) starts with your name, you identify yourself as a student, mention your class and school, and the place where you live, then you need to mention about your parents, their occupation, and as you are a student, you must have a activity that you do at free time, so you need to mention that too, so basically your introduction ends with your hobby.

For the above mentioned way of introduction, I am going to give an example below:

For example- Hi, I am Taseen Basaam and a student of class IX, I study in NORTH POINT ENGLISH ACADEMY, I live in West Bengal in a town called Malda. My father's name is MD Tabassum Ansar and my mother's name is Sabina Yeasmin, my mother is a homemaker and my father is a bussinessman. My hobbies are riding and playing video games
